Jo bol nahi paya —

Things I Never Said 🤍

"Dil mein bahut kuch tha. Alfaaz kabhi nahi aaye. Aaj aa rahe hain."

🌸

Yeh woh baatein hain jo maine kabhi nahi boli — kisi darr se, kisi waqt ki kami se, ya sirf isliye ki lagta tha — tu samajh jaayegi bina bole. Par shayad nahi samjhi. Toh aaj seedha bol raha hoon.

01 — Jo dil mein tha

Maine kabhi nahi bola — par tu mere liye kitna zyada maayane rakhti thi, woh main alfaaz mein nahi kaeh sakta tha. Teri ek message se din shuru hota tha aur teri ek message se khatam. Tu meri routine nahi thi — tu meri zaroorat thi.

🤍
02 — Tera dar

Tujhse darta tha main. Seriously. Teri ek naraazgi se dil baith jaata tha. Yeh nahi bola kabhi — kyunki lagta tha mard ko darna nahi chahiye. Par tujhse dar lagta tha — isliye ki teri importance itni zyada thi.

😅
03 — Teri hassi

Tujhe satane mein mujhe bahut mazaa aata tha. Sirf isliye nahi ki main pagal hoon — balki isliye ki jab tu chidh ke mujhe ghoorta thi — woh moment mujhe sabse zyada pasand tha. Teri chidh bhi pyaari lagti thi.

😂
04 — Teri awaaz

Tujhse baat karna — ek alag hi cheez thi. Koi bhi topic ho, teri baatein sunna mujhe kabhi boring nahi laga. Main ghanton baat kar sakta tha bina oobi. Tujhse baat karte waqt time ka hosh nahi rehta tha.

💬
05 — 2 saal baad bhi

Yeh shayad sabse badi baat hai jo kabhi seedha nahi boli — 2 saal baad bhi wahi hun main. Wahi feelings, wahi pyaar. Kuch nahi badla. Sirf itna badla ki ab bol raha hoon — kyunki aaj teri birthday hai, aur yeh din tujhe sach sunne ka haq deta hai.

🌸

"Kuch baatein dil mein rehti hain — jab tak likhi na jaayein. Aaj likh di. 🌸"
