∞ Love from Adi

Gargi,

Aaj tera birthday hai. Aur yeh sirf ek date nahi hai calendar pe — yeh woh din hai jab iss duniya mein kuch khaas hua. Jab tu aayi.

Main sochta hoon — teri smile, teri aankhein, teri woh curly hair jo tujhe tujh jaisa banati hain — aur dil mein ek feeling aati hai jo words mein nahi aati.

So today, on your birthday — I just want you to know you are loved. Deeply. Genuinely. By someone who hasn't forgotten a single thing about you.

Teri zindagi mein khushiyan aati rahe, teri muskaan kabhi na jaaye. Aur jo bhi tu chahti hai — woh sab mile tujhe. 💕

Saat rang ki duniya hai teri,
Teri hansi mein basanti bahaar. 🌸
Teri aankhon mein roshni hai itni,
Jhilmilate hain sitare baar baar. ✨

Gusse mein bhi tu lagti cute hai,
Curly baalon mein chhupa hai pyaar. 🌀
Happy Birthday Gargi, aaj ke din —
Adi ki taraf se — dil se, baar baar. 💕
