Letter to Gargi 💌

"Jo main kehna chahta tha — 2 saal se — yeh raha."

✉️
🌸 17th July, 2026 — Teri Birthday 🎂 Gargi,

Pehle yeh bata doon — yeh letter likhna aasaan nahi tha. Kai baar socha, kai baar ruka. Par aaj tera birthday hai — aur main chahta hoon ki tu jaane kuch aisi baatein jo main 2 saal se roka hua tha.

13 April 2024 se lekar 12 July 2024 tak — sirf 3 mahine. Log poochte hain — "itne kam time mein kya pyaar hoga?" Haan. Hota hai. Mujhe hua tha. Teri ek smile dekh ke — ek alag hi duniya thi.

July 12 pe jo hua — woh ek unfair ending thi. Humne kuch bura nahi kiya tha. Tu pakdi gayi ghar mein — yeh ek situation thi, reason nahi. Aur uss reason se jo humne khoya — woh kisi bhi situation se bada tha.

2 saal mein maine bahut kuch try kiya. Bhoolne ki koshish ki. Nahi hua. "Sahiba" bajti thi — tu yaad aati thi. "O Meri Laila" — tu yaad aati thi. Koi bhi cheez — teri yaad le aati thi. Kuch cheezein hoti hain jo bhoolne ke liye nahi hoti.

Gargi, aaj tera birthday hai. Aur main chahta tha ki tu jaane — tu aaj bhi meri favourite person hai. Teri curly hair, teri attitude, teri woh smile — sab kuch aaj bhi exactly wahi hai mere dil mein.

Main yeh force nahi kar raha. No pressure. Tu jaana chahti hai toh jaa — main samjhunga. Par agar tere dil mein bhi kuch hai jo abhi bhi ruka hua hai — toh woh page bhi hai is website pe. Sirf tere liye. Ek secret ke saath.

Bahut zyada likh diya. Par dil bahut bhar ke tha — sorry nahi hoon isliye. 😊

Hamesha tera,

Adi 💕
Sent with love — 17th July 2026

P.S. — "Ek secret page hai — 'Another Chance' wala. Uska ek code hai. Agar tu jaanna chahti hai woh code — toh mujhe bata de ki tu yahan tak aayi." 🔒✨
