"Yeh page likhna sabse mushkil tha — aur sabse zaroori bhi."

Gargi — tu yahan tak aayi. Matlab tune poora page dekha. Matlab kuch toh feel kiya tujhe. Aur iss page ka code — woh date thi jab humne shuru kiya tha. 13th April. Sirf tum aur main jaante hain yeh date.

2 saal bahut lambe hote hain. Logon ne kaha — "bhool ja." Songs ne kaha — "mat bhool." Main songs ki taraf gaya. Kyunki dil ko alfaaz se nahi, music se samjhaate hain.

Tu aaj bhi wahi hai jo thi uss din — meri favourite person, meri Gargi. Teri smile, teri curly hair, teri attitude, teri baatein — yeh sab list nahi hai. Yeh woh cheezein hain jo dil ne apne aap store kar li bina pooche.

July 12, 2024 — woh ending fair nahi thi. Humara pyaar khatam nahi hua tha — ek situation ne force kiya tha. Aur main sochta hoon — agar woh din na hota, toh aaj hum kahaan hote?

Shayad yeh page banana nahi padta. Shayad main seedha tujhse bolne ka mauka dhundta. Par yeh page bhi ek mauka hai — mera tera ke liye. Seedha, bina ghuma ke.

Tera Adi abhi bhi wahi hai. Wahi feelings, wahi pyaar, wahi banda jo teri ek baat sun ke sab chhod ke aata tha. Nahi badla. Shayad badal nahi sakta.
